PATH OF THE WARRIOR

Mission 3: The Logic Duel

> Status: Authorized Access

> Objective: Build a combat calculator.

THE CHALLENGE:

Your robot needs to decide whether to Attack or Defend based on its power levels. Update your solution.py to include:

  • 1. A list of 5 power levels (integers).
  • 2. A loop that checks each level.
  • 3. An if/else statement: If power > 50, print "ATTACK", otherwise print "DEFEND".
